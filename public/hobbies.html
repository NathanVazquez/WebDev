My favorite Podcasts

Armchair Expert and Unexplained

  • These are my favorite podcasts this year. Armchair expert is a podcasts that has multiple shows with in it. This podcasts interviews celebrities, and has people submit funny stories about their lives where people can meet the celebrity host to talk about an odd event that happened to them, they also have a show where a New Zealand native who now lives in the US talks about how stange he finds our customs. All the shows are very entertaining and have a unique feeling. I'll often listen to this podcast while I work or travel to Hunter.

  • Unexplained is a podcasts about unexplained mysteries. It covers stories about skinwalkers, bigfoot, missing persons cases and aliens. I started listening to this podcast in January and instantly enjoyed it. The host, Richard Maclean Smith, is an insightful narrator as he brings his listeners into towns all over the world that have experienced these unexplained mysteries.
